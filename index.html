About Dholera

India's First Planned Smart City

RSC Group Dholera, established in 2010, is a company that has been involved in the real estate industry for over 10 years.Along with real estate, the group also operates in sectors such as security, construction, and land deals.

RSC Group consists of several subsidiary companies, including RSC Realty, Dholera Triangle, RSC Buildcon, RSC Enterprise, and RSC Security. We offer Residential, Commercial and Industrial land projects as well as plot and villa with all facilities and amenities.

We help our clients with safe investments with high returns and grow their investments through strategic investment.
